2. Accessibility Service Disclosure

Rewri requests Android's Accessibility Service permission solely to:

  • Detect when you pause typing in any app (2-second idle threshold).
  • Read the text content of the active text field you are currently editing.
  • Display a floating bubble for tone selection.
  • Insert the AI-rewritten text back into the same text field upon your explicit tap.
The Accessibility Service does NOT:
  • Record keystrokes or act as a keylogger.
  • Read content from password fields (TYPE_TEXT_VARIATION_PASSWORD is excluded).
  • Access banking, payment, or financial app screens.
  • Transmit data without an explicit user tap on a tone button.
  • Operate in the background when the toggle is turned OFF.

This disclosure satisfies Google Play's Prominent Disclosure requirement under the Accessibility API policy. The Accessibility Service is used exclusively to enable the core rewriting functionality described above — not for analytics, advertising, or any secondary purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)

Your text and tone selection are transmitted over HTTPS to a Supabase Edge Function hosted at supabase.co. Supabase processes the request in memory and immediately forwards it to OpenAI. Supabase does not persist your text. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

3.2 OpenAI API

Your text is sent to OpenAI's API to generate the rewritten version. OpenAI's data use policy applies to this processing. By using Rewri you acknowledge and accept OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Usage Policies. As of the effective date of this policy, OpenAI states that API inputs are not used to train models by default. We cannot guarantee future changes to OpenAI's policy.

Do not use Rewri to rewrite: passwords, secrets, confidential legal or medical documents, personal identification numbers, or any information you would not share with a third-party AI service.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Text submitted for rewriting is processed in real-time and not stored by us after the API response is returned. Anonymised crash logs are deleted after 30 days. Aggregated analytics contain no personally identifiable information and may be retained indefinitely.

Because we do not store personal data, there is nothing to delete on request — but you may contact us at any time and we will confirm this in writing.

5. Cookies & Tracking

The Rewri mobile app does not use cookies. If you visit our website (rewri.app), standard web cookies may be used for session management and analytics. You may opt out via your browser settings. We do not use cross-site tracking, fingerprinting, or advertising trackers.

6. Children's Privacy

Rewri is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7. Security

We follow industry-standard security practices; however, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Use the App at your own risk.
